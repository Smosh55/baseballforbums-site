What to Expect

Here's how the sub and waitlist process works.

📲

Getting called as a sub: When a team is short on players for a game, we reach out to the waitlist. Sub availability is first-come, first-served. Make sure your phone number is correct.

⚾

Subbing rules: Subs play for the short-handed team and bat at the bottom of the order. Stats count. No fee for subbing.

🏆

Full roster spot: If a registered player drops out, the next eligible person on the waitlist gets first offer on the open spot.

💰

No fee to waitlist: There's no cost to join the waitlist. If you're offered a full roster spot, the $235 entry fee applies at that point.

🌧️

Rainout policy: Rained-out games are made up the following Tuesday.
